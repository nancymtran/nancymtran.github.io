Design Projects

  • Netflix Creative Jam LIVE with Adobe XD: concert

    "concert" is a music streaming web app in which users can listen to music together and chat with one another on one platform. I used Adobe XD to design and prototype the section where users are creating a new concert on their profile page.

  • Amazon Design Creative Jam LIVE with Adobe XD: Designpathy

    "Designpathy" is an educational app that introduces users to inclusive design (i.e., color accessibility) through short, visual games. I used Adobe XD to design and prototype the Splash screen, Introduction Story, Login and Signup screens, and Loading screens.

  • Flat Design creations

    I use Adobe Illustrator to create vector illustrations. I like creating flat-design illustrations the best.

    View my published illustrations on Adobe Spark.

  • Custom 404 Error Page

    I created a custom 404 error page to match my website's aethestic and personality, but I also wanted to provide users with helpful information and encourage them to explore my site further.



Research Projects

  • Undergraduate Honors Thesis

    During my senior year of college, I challenged myself to researching and writing an Honors Thesis. I compared and examined rates of interpersonal violence, post-traumatic stress symptoms, and depressive symptoms between incarcerated and non-incarcerated women.

    "TasteBuds" is a mobile app that connects people to nutritional knowledge by pairing users with other in-site (or Facebook) groups who share similar dietary needs and restrictions after taking a short personalized quiz.
